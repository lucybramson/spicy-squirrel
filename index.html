Sophie Giddens Photography

We were approached for a full design and rebuild of Sophie's site for her photography business. See here for the site, and below for some background on the project.

We defined the project requirements, then took time to understand the brand and the desired aesthetic. We used our research to advise on functionality.

Sophie's approach to photography is very natural and she wanted to achieve a calm feel for the website. After experimenting with a few options during the wireframing stage, we landed on a muted, neutral colour palette, and a minimal, irregular grid layout, making use of negative space to allow the images room to breathe.

Sophie's photography focuses on several distinct areas (from headshots to maternity to publicity images), and we worked to tie these together to make the site feel harmonious.

The majority of the copy was also provided by us.

The site is fully responsive and optimised for mobile and tablet as well as web.

We did the SEO for the site including keyword research, on-page and technical SEO, with some photography-specific SEO best practices implemented.

The logo and all graphic elements were produced by Spicy Squirrel, including the various icons and banners used for cross-platform sharing.

Doggle

Doggle is an Supergalactic Canine Snap Game, created by Spicy Squirrel. The game is set in a galaxy far far away, where there are two planets, with many dogs spinning around. The aim of the game is to match the dogs! click here to play!

Spicy Squirrel developed the game from end to end - from concept, through design and development. We developed the idea, hand coded the game and the animations, and proudly designed the 70s arcade aesthetic and the unexplained outer space theme.
