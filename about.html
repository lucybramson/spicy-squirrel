Spicy Squirrel was founded in 2023 by Lucy B, built on over 10 years engineering experience and a strong creative vision.
‍
At Spicy Squirrel we value independence, creativity and flexibility, and we bring you on the journey with us. We are strategic thinkers, and the work we do extends beyond the typical client/designer/developer relationship.

We understand that sending a developer off into the sunset with a vague brief won’t achieve your desired result. That's why we've developed a process that promotes regular communication and review, so there are no nasty surprises.

We're flexible in terms of your level of creative input and passionate about creating something that makes you stand out. Your end result will feel slick and unique.
‍
Spicy Squirrel is all about cultivating an idea from seed and adding a little spice as it grows.
